The Brewing Process
🫘

Harvest

Read local histories from Claude Code, Codex, Copilot Chat, Cursor, and LM Studio. Nothing leaves your machine.

⚙️

Sort

Keep human-authored prompts. Remove system messages, tool output, and assistant text from scoring.

🔥

Roast

Embed each prompt with bge-small-en-v1.5 locally. Cosine similarity to reference clusters scores 7 metrics.

Brew

Four axes — Precision, Curiosity, Tenacity, Trust — translate into the persona that fits your pattern.
