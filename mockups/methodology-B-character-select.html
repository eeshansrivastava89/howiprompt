Pipeline

Collect

Read local histories from Claude Code, Codex, Copilot Chat, Cursor, and LM Studio.

2

Extract

Keep human-authored prompts. Remove system messages, tool output, and assistant text.

3

Embed each prompt with bge-small-en-v1.5 locally. Cosine similarity scores 7 metrics in one pass.

4

Classify

Four axes — Precision, Curiosity, Tenacity, Trust — determine the persona that fits your pattern.
