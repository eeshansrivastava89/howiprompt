The score types are grouped to feel more like a decoder than a report.

Each category answers a distinct question: how much you prompt, when you work, how you steer the AI, and which axis dominates your overall pattern.

Volume

How much you engage

These are the straightforward throughput signals that anchor the rest of the experience.

PromptsTotal human-authored messages across all included histories.
ConversationsDistinct threads or sessions you started or participated in.
WordsTotal words typed by you across those prompts.
Timing

When your prompting happens

Temporal signals make it easier to spot patterns in rhythm, consistency, and late-night work.

Peak hour / dayThe times when your prompting activity is most concentrated.
Night owlThe share of prompts sent during late-night hours.
Activity trendHow your prompt volume changes over time.
Style scores

How you steer the model

These scores describe the way you work with the AI, not just what you ask it to do.

Human in the LoopHow actively you review, guide, and shape outputs.
Vibe Coder IndexWhether your style feels more structured or more improvisational.
PolitenessHow warm, courteous, and collaborative your tone tends to be.
Persona axes

The four traits behind personas

These are the dimensions that shape the persona system and explain why someone lands where they do.

PrecisionHow detailed and constrained your instructions usually are.
CuriosityHow much you explore, compare, and ask open-ended questions.
TenacityHow deeply you stay with one problem before moving on.
TrustHow willing you are to let the model fill in the path for you.
