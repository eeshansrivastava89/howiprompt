How it works
01

Detect & sync

Read supported local histories from Claude Code, Codex, Copilot Chat, Cursor, and LM Studio.

02

Extract prompts

Keep human-authored prompts and remove system chatter, tool output, and assistant text from scoring.

03

Score patterns

Use embeddings to summarize style, intent, and prompting behavior without brittle keyword rules.

04

Assign persona

Translate the strongest axis into a persona that describes the overall pattern of how you prompt.

What gets scored

Volume

Timing

Style scores

Precision, Curiosity, Tenacity, and Trust drive persona assignment.
