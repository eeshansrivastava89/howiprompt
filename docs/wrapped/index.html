☕ Methodology

From raw chats to your signature blend

We harvest your local chat history, roast it through embeddings, and brew a persona that captures how you prompt.

The Brewing Process
🫘

Harvest

Read local histories from Claude Code, Codex, Copilot Chat, Cursor, and LM Studio.

⚙️

Sort

Keep human-authored prompts. Remove system messages, tool output, and assistant text.

🔥

Roast

Embed each prompt with bge-small-en-v1.5 locally. Score 7 metrics via cosine similarity.

☕

Brew

Two axes — Detail Level and Communication Style — place you on a 2×2 grid that maps to your persona.
