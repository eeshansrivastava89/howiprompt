Methodology

How It Works

1

Data Collection

Reads local Claude Code, Codex, Copilot Chat, Cursor, and LM Studio histories from your machine. Sync and analysis stay local unless you explicitly opt into leaderboard submission.

2

Message Extraction

Filters to human-authored prompts. Excludes system commands, tool outputs, and agent sessions.

3

Embedding & Classification

Each prompt is embedded with bge-small-en-v1.5 (local, offline). Cosine similarity to reference clusters scores 7 metrics in a single pass.

4

Persona Classification

Four radar axes (Precision, Curiosity, Tenacity, Trust) are computed from embeddings. The dominant axis maps you to one of 5 personas.

All Metrics

Volume

Prompts — your messages · Conversations — chat sessions · Words — total typed

Temporal

Peak Hour/Day — most active · Night Owl — 11pm-4am % · Activity — prompts/week trend

Hero Scores (Embedding)

Human in the Loop — how much you steer AI output. Higher = more hands-on review and iteration.
Vibe Coder Index — engineer vs vibe coder. Higher = more structured, spec-driven prompting.
Politeness — tone warmth. Higher = more courteous and collaborative.

Radar Axes (Embedding)

Precision — how detailed your instructions are
Curiosity — do you explore or execute?
Tenacity — how deep you go on one problem
Trust — how much you delegate to the AI

The 5 Personas

Derived from your 4 radar axes. The dominant dimension determines your persona:

The Architect High Precision, low Trust. Every prompt is a spec, every output gets reviewed.
The Explorer High Curiosity. You ask questions, dig into details, and follow threads wherever they lead.
The Commander High Precision, low Tenacity. Precise orders in short bursts — no wasted words.
The Partner High Tenacity. Long collaborative sessions, iterating until it's right.
The Delegator High Trust. You paint the vision and let AI handle the details.

All 7 metrics use embedding-based classification — no regex or keyword matching.
